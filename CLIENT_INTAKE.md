# Client Intake Checklist

Collect this before building a real contractor site.

## Business Details

- Business name
- Owner/contact name
- Phone number
- Email address
- City/state
- Service area
- Business hours
- Preferred call-to-action

## Services

- Main services
- Services they do not want to advertise
- Best project types
- Typical project size
- Emergency or after-hours availability, if any

## Brand Assets

- Logo
- Brand colors
- Existing website, if any
- Social links
- Google Business Profile link

## Photos

- Approved project photos
- Before/after photos
- Crew photos, if approved
- Vehicle or storefront photos, if approved
- Written permission to use every photo

Recommended sizes:

- Hero: `1600x1000` or larger
- Gallery: `900x675`

Recommended naming:

- `hero-business-name.jpg`
- `gallery-kitchen-remodel.jpg`
- `gallery-flooring-install.jpg`
- `gallery-concrete-patio.jpg`

## Testimonials and Claims

- Approved testimonials/reviews
- Source of each review
- License proof if they want licensed claims shown
- Insurance proof if they want insured claims shown
- Warranty/guarantee wording, if any
- Years in business proof, if they want it shown

Do not publish these claims without proof or written approval.

## Domain and Accounts

- Domain preference
- Existing registrar login owner
- Hosting preference
- Form email recipient
- Analytics approval

Use 1Password to store client/domain credentials safely. Do not store passwords in the repository.
